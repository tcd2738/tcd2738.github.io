Fighter Updates:

Welcome to the first annual Virtual Hunger Games!

Add custom fighters through the Fighter Submission form, or add premade ones through the Randomized Fighter button.

You can also control the layout of your arena by choosing a terrain in the Terrain Type dropdown. Slower fighters may have a tougher time overcoming more difficult terrains!

Once you have finished adding fighters, hit the Start button.

See arena news in the Fighter Update section, and check on specific fighters in the fighter table.

You may change the game speed using the Speed Controls, or pause the game at any point using the Pause button.

Refresh the page to start again.

May the odds be ever in your favor!
